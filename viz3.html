Cloudiness

Of the metrics observed, cloudiness correlates to latitude the least. This might be an indication that cloudiness is a more chaotic phenomena than any of the other metrics, or it might be that cloudiness correlates to other metrics such as rainfall more tightly. It would be interesting to observe how cloudiness matches wind speed.
